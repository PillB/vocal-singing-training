Privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-07-16 · Product: Vocal Studio (PillB) · Host: GitHub Pages

1. What this app is

Vocal Studio is a browser-based vocal and singing practice tool. Core practice runs locally in your browser. There is no account server for everyday practice data.

2. Data stored on your device

We store practice progress, settings, and optional billing entitlement flags in localStorage / sessionStorage on your device (for example progress, streaks, language, soft Pro trial). Clearing site data removes it. We do not operate a central database of your exercise scores.

3. Microphone & audio

The microphone is used only when you start practice, for pitch feedback and optional recording. Audio processing happens in the browser. We do not upload your voice to our servers (there is no such backend on the static site).

4. Payments

Paid Pro checkout is handled by Stripe and/or Mercado Pago on their hosted pages. Card details never touch our origin. See provider privacy policies for payment data.

5. Optional ads / studio tips (free tier)

Free-tier studio tips (native cards or, if enabled later, display ads) may appear only outside live practice. They are off by default until the operator enables them. Pro and trial users do not see these tips/ads.

If Google AdSense or similar networks are enabled later, those third parties may set cookies or use device identifiers for ad delivery and measurement under their own policies. Affiliate links may be labeled as sponsored.

6. Analytics

Optional local analytics events may be recorded in the browser for product improvement (for example session counts). No personal identity is required to practice.

7. Third-party links

Links to external sites (payment providers, affiliate partners, documentation) are governed by those sites’ policies. We are not responsible for their content.

8. Children

The app is a general skill practice tool. If you are a parent/guardian, supervise use of the microphone and any external links.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy: open an issue on the project repository (PillB/vocal-singing-training) or contact the site operator via the repository profile.

10. Changes

We may update this page when monetization, hosting, or data practices change. The “Last updated” date will move when material changes are made.
